Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Overview

Fake Call ("the App") is developed by Cloud Motion Lab LLC. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter in the App (caller names, phone numbers, photos, presets) is stored locally on your device only.

Voice and Audio Files

Voice scenario audio and ringtone files are bundled within the App. No audio is recorded, uploaded, or shared.

Microphone Access

The App does not access your device microphone.

Photos

If you choose to add a contact photo, the selected image is stored locally on your device. It is never uploaded or shared with any third party.

Advertising

The App uses Unity Ads to display advertisements to free users. Unity Ads may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policy. You can remove all ads by purchasing Fake Call Pro.

Subscriptions and Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics services. We may receive anonymized, aggregated data from Apple through App Store Connect.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
